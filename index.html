Preparing for Your Scan

Follow these important guidelines to ensure the most accurate and comprehensive scan results.

Do These Before Your Scan

  • Arrive 15 minutes early for check-in and preparation

  • Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing

  • Stay well-hydrated in the days leading up to your scan

  • Get adequate sleep the night before

  • Bring a list of current medications and supplements

Important Restrictions

  • No alcohol consumption 24 hours prior to scan

  • Remove all jewelry, watches, and metal accessories

  • No caffeine 4 hours before your appointment

  • Pacemakers and electronic implants are not compatible

  • Avoid heavy meals 2 hours before scanning

Booking & Cancellation Policy

We maintain professional standards that honor both your time and the needs of our community.

Booking Requirements

Deposit: A $50 non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment, applied toward your total session cost.

Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email with detailed preparation instructions within 24 hours of booking.

Scheduling: Appointments are available Tuesday through Saturday, with morning and afternoon time slots.

Cancellation Policy

48-Hour Notice: Full refund minus deposit with 48+ hours advance notice.

24-Hour Notice: 50% refund with 24-48 hours notice to allow rebooking opportunity.

Same-Day: No refund for same-day cancellations or no- shows, as this prevents other clients from accessing care.

Our Commitment to Community Care

We understand that life circumstances can change unexpectedly. Our policies are designed to balance compassion with responsibility to our entire community of clients seeking healing. In cases of genuine emergency or health concerns, we work with individuals on a case-by-case basis to find fair solutions. We believe that honoring commitments and respecting each other's time creates a foundation for the healing work we do together.
